About me

A story of innovation and growth

Ever since I was a kid, I’ve been curious about how things work. I used to build little experiments—like homemade helicopters, testing remote control signal ranges, or exploring game ideas I had in my head. For me, science wasn’t just something from books—it was something to play with, test, and bring to life. As I grew up, that curiosity turned into a passion for technology. I started exploring computers, learning how software works, and getting into programming. I didn’t just want to use apps—I wanted to understand how they were built and maybe one day, build my own.

That’s when I discovered the world of AI and machine learning. Now, I’m focused on learning how to build smart systems, write clean code, and bring my own tech ideas to life. I’m currently teaching frontend web design and tracking production assets at Holly Heart Global School, Samalkha, while finishing my BCA degree under Kurukshetra University. I enjoy working on challenges that push me to think creatively.
